BISHOP DAVID OZEDEPO

The founder of Living Faith church Worldwide, and presiding Bishop of Faith Terbanacle in Ota,Ogun State

  • Oyedepo has been seen as one of the pioneers of the christian charismatic movement in Africa and has been reffered to as one of the most powerful preachers in Nigeria
  • Bishop Oyedepo was instructed by God to build a new base for the commission to accommodate the increasing number of worshippers known as cannanland which is reputed to be the largest church auditorium in the world as recorded by the Guinness Book of Records in December 2005
  • He is the chancellor of Convenant University and Landmark University
  • He was named in the 2011 Forbes magazine as being the richest pastor in Nigeria
  • Ozedepo is the president of David Oyedepo Ministries International(DOMI)
  • As with many of his influences, Oyedepo has been discribed as a preacher of the prosperity gospel
  • The ministry is currently building a 100,000 seater auditorium called "the Ark", which is reputed to be the building with the largest span in the world
  • He is the chairman/publisher of Dominion Publishing House(DPH), a publishing arm of the ministry
  • The church owns four private jets and several buildings, including in London and the US
  • Oyedepo is an author and publisher who has written over 70 titles apart from periodicals
  • Oyedepo has criticized curruption in Africa as a whole and poor leadership in government
